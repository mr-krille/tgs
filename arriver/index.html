About this project

The concept of arriver is to simplify the way you meet with someone. After you’ve sent an arriver to your partner & he accepts it, he will receive information about your location as well as an estimated time of arrival. The goal of The Gentleman Studio was to create an app with this functionality - as simple and attractive as possible - from concept development to final design drafts.

The Challenge

The biggest challenge was to guide the user through the app with only a few steps, self-explanatory and really easy to use. To create a simple communication between the partners meeting we also had to find a nice way displaying distance and speed.
