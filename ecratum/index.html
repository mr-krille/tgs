About this project

Ecratum offers a handy web application for companies to keep up in a better relationship with their suppliers. Focus of this web app is to digitize printed data and one of the results doing this is work & time minimization. The Gentleman Studio was commissioned for a complete redesign of the existing online presence as well as a refresh of the corporate identity.

The Challenge

The intended target group is defined by a higher age in average and usually little affinity for digital products. Thus, the major challenge was to create a pleasant mixture between seriousness and humanity.

The Facts

  • Amount of finished screens: about 20
