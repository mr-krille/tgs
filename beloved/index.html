About this project

As a youthful fashion label for individual campus collections and sports clothing The Gentleman Studio was commissioned by beloved for creating an unique corporate identity. Due to strong print usage of the image / word mark, of course a lot of things had to be considered but for us the result is as simple as ingenious.

The Challenge

The big challenge was to stand out from the identity of standard labels and despite a trendy style to still hold a sense of honesty and quality.

The Facts

  • Designed in spring 2014
  • Corporate Identity incl. Guide as well as print equipment
  • Setting up an online store on Magento
