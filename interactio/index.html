About this project

Every day countless people are dealing with customers & sales stuff. Many of them dream of a better solution to manage the relationship with their customers. It starts with the processing of daily tasks and goes up to displaying targeted customer information when calling someone, which would be very useful. This was our case. The task of the Gentleman Studio was issued to find a better solution for sales agents which enables them to keep up a better management on their daily business & to use in-app intelligence for a better work flow as well as work reduction.

The Challenge

Simplicity in the application should be given despite enormous complexity and functionality. The user should recognize the intelligence by a pleasant integration and thereby realize significant additional value while working through the day.

The Facts

  • Designed in summer 2014
  • Amount of finished screens: about 150
