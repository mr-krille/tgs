About this project

Partech Ventures is a venture capital firm with $650 million under active management, located in San Francisco, Paris & Berlin. This are good reasons for being one of the most attractive VC for entrepreneurs from all over the world. The Gentleman Studio was commissioned to create a professional web presence & thus we set our goal to a level where Partech Ventures will be represented with all of their sympathy, corporate values, but also their market status in an individual & unique webdesign.

The Challenge

The complex design concept with all the slopes brought a little challenge. As we had also the care of active third party content integration, updates on pages such as portfolio had to be as simple as possible.

The Facts

  • Designed in summer 2014
  • Amount of final screens: about 8
  • responsive for mobile, tablet, notebooks & big screens
